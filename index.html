About me

My name is John McCombs, and I am a Ph.D. student in Civil Engineering at the University of Central Florida. My research focuses on improving traffic operations and safety in Florida using statistical modeling and data science. I have experience working with wrong-way driving, safety performance functions, and traffic incident management. My most recent project, which is the subject of my Ph.D., is developing a corridor safety approach using both crash and non-crash data to identify high-risk suburban and urban roadways where crashes and fatalities can be reduced.
